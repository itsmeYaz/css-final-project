Projects

Infrastructure Development: Rimuru prioritizes the construction of essential infrastructure within Tempest, such as roads, bridges, and buildings. This infrastructure not only facilitates the movement of goods and people but also enhances the overall quality of life for residents.

Diplomatic Relations: Rimuru actively seeks diplomatic relations with neighboring nations and races. He engages in negotiations, trade agreements, and alliances to promote peace and cooperation in the region. Rimuru's diplomatic efforts play a crucial role in establishing Tempest as a respected and influential nation.
